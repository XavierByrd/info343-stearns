Page Title

The page-level navigation bar on the left has a fixed width, but the article surrounding this content expands to fill the rest of the viewport. It uses a left margin that is a littel bigger than the width of the left nav so that it doesn't wrap around the shorter nav.

The bunny image adjusts its size as the viewport gets more narrow, but it also has max-width and min-width values to keep it from getting ridiculously big or small.
