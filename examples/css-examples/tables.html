Styling Tables

By default, browsers don't do much when formatting tables. Table headings (th) are usually centered and rendered in bold, but that's about it. But you can make your tables look much nicer by applying a bit of CSS. Like the other example pages, right click and choose "View Page Source" to see how this is done.
